# Facebook Ad Creative Prompts for TrackerDelivery

## Overview
5 ChatGPT-5 prompts designed to create Facebook ad banners for TrackerDelivery's customer acquisition campaign targeting foreign F&B owners in Bali.

---

## 🚨 ПРОМПТ #1: ДРАМА И ПОТЕРИ

Create a Facebook ad banner (1080x1080px) for a restaurant monitoring service in Bali. 

**Visual concept**: Split-screen design showing BEFORE/AFTER scenario:
- **LEFT SIDE**: Frustrated foreign restaurant owner (European/Australian man, 35-40) looking at phone with shocked expression, red "CLOSED" notification visible on screen
- **RIGHT SIDE**: Same person smiling, looking at green "MONITORED" dashboard on phone

**TEXT OVERLAY**:
- Main headline: "Your Restaurant Lost $200 Yesterday"
- Subheading: "You didn't even know it was closed"
- CTA button: "Get 14-Day FREE Trial"

**DESIGN STYLE**:
- Modern, clean design with Bali tropical background (blurred palm trees)
- Color scheme: Red for problem side, green for solution side
- Professional typography, readable on mobile
- Include small logos: GrabFood, GoFood at bottom
- Company name: "TrackerDelivery" in bottom corner

**TARGET**: Foreign F&B owners in Bali who lose revenue from platform closures

---

## 🔥 ПРОМПТ #2: СРОЧНОСТЬ И АВТОМАТИЗАЦИЯ

Design a Facebook ad banner (1200x628px landscape) for restaurant delivery monitoring.

**Visual concept**: Phone mockup showing real-time alerts with timeline:
- Large smartphone in center showing WhatsApp-style notifications
- Notification bubbles: "🚨 Your restaurant went offline 2 minutes ago"
- Background: Blurred Bali restaurant scene (Canggu/Seminyak vibe)
- Clock showing different times with "INSTANT ALERTS" text

**TEXT ELEMENTS**:
- Main headline: "Never Lose Another Order"
- Secondary: "Get alerted in 5 minutes when GrabFood closes your restaurant"
- Value prop: "Foreign owners in Bali save $150/day on average"
- CTA: "Start FREE 14-Day Trial"

**VISUAL STYLE**:
- Mobile-first design with bright notification colors
- Modern gradient background (blue to teal, Bali-inspired)
- Clean, minimal icons and typography
- Sense of urgency through color and movement
- Small "For Foreign Restaurant Owners" text at bottom

## 💰 ПРОМПТ #3: ROI И КОНКРЕТНЫЕ ЦИФРЫ

Create a Facebook ad banner (1080x1080px square) focused on ROI for restaurant owners.

**Visual concept**: Calculator/dashboard mockup showing savings:
- Large digital display showing: "$2,100 SAVED THIS MONTH"
- Small icons showing prevented issues: closed restaurant, menu errors, bad reviews
- Professional businessman (foreign expat) in background, slightly blurred
- Bali restaurant exterior in soft background

**TEXT STRUCTURE**:
- Headline: "Track Every Dollar You're Losing"
- Supporting text: "$49/month saves $2,100/month"
- Benefit points (small text):
  • Instant closure alerts
  • Menu error detection  
  • Review monitoring
- CTA button: "Calculate Your Savings - FREE Trial"

**DESIGN APPROACH**:
- Clean, business-focused color scheme (navy blue, white, gold accents)
- Professional charts/graphs elements
- Money/calculator iconography
- Trustworthy, corporate feel
- Target: serious business owners who think in ROI terms

## ⚡ ПРОМПТ #5: ПРОБЛЕМА + РЕШЕНИЕ (ТРАНСФОРМАЦИЯ)

Create a Facebook ad banner (1080x1350px - Instagram story format) showing transformation.

**COPY ELEMENTS**:
- Opening hook: "This Happened to 78 Restaurants Last Week"
- Problem: "Platforms closed them without warning"
- Solution: "TrackerDelivery prevents this"
- Social proof: "Join 200+ protected restaurants in Bali"
- CTA: "Get Protected FREE for 14 Days"

**DESIGN STYLE**:
- Vertical storytelling format optimized for mobile
- Clear visual progression from problem to solution
- Contrasting emotions (stress → relief)
- Modern app interface mockups
- Strong call-to-action at bottom

## 📋 ДОПОЛНИТЕЛЬНЫЕ ИНСТРУКЦИИ ДЛЯ ВСЕХ ПРОМПТОВ

### ТЕХНИЧЕСКИЕ ТРЕБОВАНИЯ:
- High-resolution output suitable for Facebook ads
- Mobile-optimized text (readable on small screens)
- Facebook ad compliance (no misleading claims)
- Include TrackerDelivery branding
- Target audience: English-speaking expats in Bali

### ЭМОЦИОНАЛЬНЫЕ ТРИГГЕРЫ:
- Fear of losing money (FOMO)
- Relief from automation
- Professional credibility
- Local Bali connection
- Immediate gratification (free trial)

### ОБЯЗАТЕЛЬНЫЕ ЭЛЕМЕНТЫ:
- "14-Day FREE Trial" mention
- Target platforms: GrabFood, GoFood  
- Geographic focus: Bali
- Price point context ($49/month)
- Urgency/scarcity elements
